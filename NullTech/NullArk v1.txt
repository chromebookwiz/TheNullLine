## PART ONE: WHY GRAVITY IS THE RIGHT PROPULSION

### 1.1 The Problem with Every Other Method [ESTABLISHED]

Every propulsion method we've discussed so far has a fundamental limitation for interstellar travel:

The common problem: all reaction-based propulsion requires carrying propellant (mass that you throw backward to go forward). The question is: can you fabricate a gravitational field?

### 2.4 Wormholes in the Null Framework [MATHEMATICAL]

A wormhole (Einstein-Rosen bridge) is a topological tunnel in spacetime connecting two distant regions. 5. Sedation is gradually withdrawn. The colonist wakes over ~30 minutes, groggy but intact. The pod opens.

## PART FOUR: THE ONBOARD GRAVITY RESEARCH PROGRAMME

The Ark carries the E₈ Casimir experiment package from the Null Disc specification, plus additional equipment for gravitational research during the 50-year cruise. This is not propulsion — it is basic science that might, over generations, lead to gravitational engineering.

### 4.1 Experiment: High-Energy-Density Null Confinement

**Purpose:** Determine whether sufficiently intense null ray confinement (photons trapped in ultra-high-Q resonators) produces measurable spacetime curvature.

**Setup:** A single CaF₂ microsphere (Q > 10¹², fabricated by the onboard Forge) pumped with maximum circulating optical power. At Q = 10¹² and input power 1 W, the circulating power inside the sphere is:

P_circ = P_input × Q / (2π) ≈ 1.6 × 10¹¹ W

The energy stored in the sphere:

E_stored = P_circ × τ_round-trip = 1.6 × 10¹¹ × 1.5 × 10⁻¹² ≈ 0.24 J

The equivalent mass:

m_equiv = E/c² ≈ 2.7 × 10⁻¹⁸ kg

The gravitational field of this mass at the sphere's surface (R = 50 μm):

g = Gm/R² = 6.67 × 10⁻¹¹ × 2.7 × 10⁻¹⁸ / (50 × 10⁻⁶)² = 7.2 × 10⁻²⁰ m/s²

This is unmeasurably small with any current technology. But the experiment isn't looking for the absolute gravitational field — it's looking for the *anomalous* field: any deviation from the predicted value that correlates with the null billiard orbit structure (the E₈ mode spectrum of the sphere).

**Measurement:** Atom interferometry. Cold rubidium atoms are dropped past the microsphere. The gravitational phase shift on the atomic wavefunction is:

Δφ = (m_Rb × g × T²) / ħ

For T = 1 s (free fall time in the ship's microgravity environment during cruise — the rotating habitat provides gravity at the rim, but the ship's spine is near-zero-g): Δφ ≈ 10⁻²⁰ rad. Unmeasurable.

**But:** If 10⁶ microspheres are arranged coherently (all pumped in phase, E₈ lattice coupling), the equivalent mass scales to ~2.7 × 10⁻¹² kg, the field to ~7.2 × 10⁻¹⁴ m/s², and the phase shift to ~10⁻¹⁴ rad. Still small, but atom interferometers have achieved sensitivities of ~10⁻¹² m/s² (Kasevich group, Stanford). Within 2–3 orders of magnitude. Over 50 years of cruise time, the onboard Forge fabricates progressively higher-Q microspheres and larger arrays, pushing toward detectability.

**What we're really looking for [CONJECTURAL]:** The null framework predicts that the gravitational field of confined photons depends not just on the total energy but on the ADE type of the confinement. A set of photons confined in an E₈-symmetric arrangement might (if the framework's deepest suggestions are correct) produce a different gravitational signature than the same energy confined randomly. This is the experimental test of Open Problem P7: does null geometry directly generate gravity?

### 4.2 Experiment: Casimir Wormhole Seed

**Purpose:** Determine whether a sufficiently intense and precisely structured Casimir cavity produces any topological anomaly in the local spacetime geometry.

**Setup:** A stack of 10,000 Null Forge-fabricated Casimir cavities (gold plates, d = 1 nm gap, 100 μm × 100 μm area), producing a total Casimir energy of:

= 10,000 × (−1.3 × 10⁸ Pa) × (10⁻⁴)² m² × 10⁻⁹ m

= 10,000 × (−1.3 × 10⁻⁹ J)

## PART FIVE: THE RING OF SINGULARITIES — A MATHEMATICAL DESIGN

This section is tagged entirely [MATHEMATICAL / CONJECTURAL]. It describes what a ring of micro-singularities would do if they could be created. They cannot be created with any foreseeable technology. We include this because the mathematics is valid, the physical consequences are computable, and the structure illuminates the null framework's deepest implications.

### 5.1 The Kerr Ring

A rotating black hole (Kerr metric) contains a ring singularity — not a point, but a ring of zero thickness and finite radius. The ring radius is:

r_ring = a = J/(Mc)

where J is the angular momentum and M is the mass. The ring singularity has extraordinary properties:

- Passing through the plane of the ring (from above to below) does NOT hit the singularity (which has zero thickness). Instead, it takes you to a different region of spacetime — the inner Kerr geometry, which connects to other universes in the maximal analytic extension.
- The ring acts as a gravitational lens of extreme power. Light passing near the ring is deflected by arbitrary angles, including multiple full orbits.
- The ergosphere (a region outside the event horizon where spacetime is dragged by the rotation) allows energy extraction via the Penrose process: throw matter into the ergosphere on a retrograde orbit, and it comes out with MORE energy than it went in with, extracted from the black hole's rotational energy. This is [ESTABLISHED] general relativity.

### 5.2 An Artificial Ring of Micro-Singularities

Instead of a single Kerr black hole (which requires stellar-mass compression), consider a ring of N much smaller singularities arranged in a circle:

**Configuration:** N micro-singularities, each of mass m, arranged in a ring of radius R, equally spaced at angular separation 2π/N. Each singularity orbits the ring's centre at velocity v = √(GM_total/(R)) to maintain circular motion.

In the null billiard language: this is an A_{N-1} periodic orbit in the gravitational potential of the ring itself. The N singularities are the N reflection points of a null billiard with q = N. The composite four-momentum of the ring has K·K = −(Nm)² (the ring's total mass squared). The ADE type is A_{N-1} — the simplest cyclic classification.

**Gravitational properties of the ring (computed from Newtonian gravity + post-Newtonian corrections for the near-field, full GR for the strong-field region near each singularity):**

**On the axis (above/below the ring):** The gravitational field points toward the ring plane. At height z above the centre:

g_axis(z) = GNmz / (R² + z²)^{3/2}

Maximum at z = R/√2, where g_max = 2GNm/(3√3 R²).

**In the plane, at the centre of the ring:** Zero gravity — all forces cancel by symmetry. This is a saddle point: stable in the plane, unstable perpendicular to the plane. A ship at the ring's centre floats in zero-g but must actively maintain position against axial perturbations.

**Near a single singularity:** The field is dominated by that singularity's Schwarzschild geometry. Tidal forces become extreme at r < r_s = 2Gm/c².

### 5.3 The Ring as a Drive

If the ring could be fabricated and positioned, it provides propulsive force to any mass near it:

**Configuration for travel:** The ring of singularities is positioned ahead of the ship (like the asteroid in Section 2.2). The ship falls toward the ring's centre. As the ship approaches the centre (where gravity is zero), the ring is shifted further ahead (by accelerating the individual singularities along their orbits — changing the ring's centre of mass position). The ship perpetually falls toward a receding target.

**How to move the ring:** This is the key problem. Options:

1. **Penrose process [ESTABLISHED GR, CONJECTURAL engineering]:** If the individual singularities are spinning (Kerr micro-black-holes), energy and momentum can be extracted from their rotation by directing matter or radiation through their ergospheres. The extracted momentum shifts the ring. This is propellantless in principle — the "fuel" is the rotational energy of the singularities themselves.

2. **Hawking radiation asymmetry [ESTABLISHED physics, CONJECTURAL engineering]:** Micro-black-holes emit Hawking radiation at temperature T_H = ħc³/(8πGMk_B). For a 10⁹ kg black hole, T_H ≈ 1.2 × 10¹⁴ K — extremely hot, radiating ~3.6 × 10²⁰ W. If a mirror or metamaterial reflector (Null Forge-fabricated, naturally) is placed on one side of the singularity, the Hawking radiation on that side is reflected back, producing a net thrust in the opposite direction. This is a Hawking radiation thruster — converting the singularity's mass directly into directed thrust with perfect efficiency (E = mc²).

3. **Gravitational wave emission [ESTABLISHED physics]:** The ring of singularities in circular orbits emits gravitational waves, carrying away energy and angular momentum. The ring slowly spirals inward. By controlling which singularities are closer or farther (modulating the ring shape), the gravitational wave emission can be made asymmetric, producing net thrust. This is the gravitational analogue of photonic propulsion.

### 5.4 The Ring as a Wormhole Frame [CONJECTURAL]

The most speculative application: a ring of singularities might, if properly configured, create a traversable wormhole through its centre.

**The mathematical argument:** The Kerr metric's ring singularity connects to other spacetime regions through its interior. A ring of discrete singularities approximates a Kerr ring in the limit N → ∞. For finite N, the throat geometry is more complex but topologically similar: the interior of the ring has a different causal structure from the exterior.

**The physical obstacle:** Even if the topology permits a wormhole, traversability requires the throat to remain open long enough for a ship to pass through. This requires exotic matter (negative energy density) at the throat. The Casimir effect between the singularities provides some negative energy, but (as computed in Section 2.4) the magnitude is vastly insufficient.

**A potential way around the obstacle [CONJECTURAL]:** The null framework suggests that the Riemann zero spectrum of the Casimir energy might produce resonance enhancement in specific geometric configurations. If the ring of singularities is embedded in an 8-dimensional configuration (with 240 singularities at E₈ root positions, projected down to a 3D ring), the resonance enhancement might amplify the Casimir negative energy by the required 40+ orders of magnitude.

This is the framework's most extreme conjecture. There is no physics derivation supporting 40 orders of magnitude of resonance enhancement. The mathematical hint — that E₈ optimises information density and theta series coefficients — does not constitute a physical prediction. We state it for completeness and because it is testable in principle (the Ark's gravity experiments, run for decades, might detect precursor effects).
